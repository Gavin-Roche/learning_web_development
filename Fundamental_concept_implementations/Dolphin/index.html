MDS - What we do

MSD was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate marine enthusiasts from a diverse range of backgrounds. We are a non-profit organisation that promotes the research and conservation of the dolphin population in Irish waters. We rely solely on grants, fund raising and public donations.

We work with local communities both at home and abroad to protect these amazing creatures and the places where they live. Our Coastwatch programme allows community volunteers to collect important sightings data from around the Irish coastline. Our latest research has led to a proposal to create a marine protected area off the coast of Kerry.

We are also involved in the fight against plastic and pollution to create healthy seas. We are working closely with both the Irish and UK governments to protect dolphins and their habitats. We continue to raise awareness around the continued contamination of our seas from dangerous chemicals such as PCBs (PolyChlorinated Biphenyls). PCBs build up in the food chain and become toxic to top predators such as dolphins.
